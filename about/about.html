Who We Are

Antra Enterprises is a multi-disciplinary firm based in Bhagalpur, Bihar, dedicated to providing high-quality industrial and operational solutions. Established on Amarpur Road, we have grown into a trusted partner for both government and private sectors.

We specialize in the supply, management, and maintenance of critical infrastructure components. Our expertise spans across D.G. Set operations, specialized civil constructions, and uninterrupted medical oxygen plant management.

Our GSTIN-registered company (10AMZPK6153B1Z6) maintains the highest standards of compliance and professional service delivery, making us the preferred choice for hospitals and enterprises across Bihar.
