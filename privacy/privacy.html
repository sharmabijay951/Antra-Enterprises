Privacy Policy for Antra Enterprises

Effective Date: May 2026 | Last Updated: May 2026

At Antra Enterprises, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and professional information when you use our website or apply for a job through our Careers Portal.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a job application on our Careers Portal, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Full Name, WhatsApp Mobile Number, Age, and Residential Address.
  • Professional Details: Highest Qualification, Work Experience, and the Post you are applying for.
  • Mandatory KYC Documents: Passport Size Photo, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and Bank Passbook/Cheque.
  • Educational Documents: Marksheets and Degree Certificates (10th, 12th, Graduation, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for recruitment and verification purposes. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Review your application for current or future job vacancies at Antra Enterprises.
  • Perform background and identity checks using your KYC documents.
  • Communicate with you regarding your application status, interview schedules, or job offers via WhatsApp or call.
  • Maintain internal HR records.

3. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to keeping your data secure. All application data and uploaded documents are directly transmitted to and stored on our highly secure, encrypted cloud servers (Google Drive and Google Workspace). We implement strict access controls, meaning only authorized HR personnel and senior management of Antra Enterprises have access to your data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or KYC documents to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may only be shared with:

  • Government or law enforcement authorities, if required by law.
  • Our affiliated hospitals or client sites strictly for deployment verification, with your prior consent.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your application data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. If your application is rejected, we may keep your details on file for future opportunities. You have the right to request the deletion or correction of your data at any time by contacting our HR department.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
